About Northwin Global City

Welcome to Northwin Global City, a premier destination poised to transform Bulacan's landscape into a vibrant hub for commerce and leisure. Situated just 20 kilometers north of Metro Manila along the North Luzon Expressway (NLEX), our dynamic development is set to redefine the region's economic and cultural identity. From themed commercial districts to high-rise residential condominiums, upscale hotels, and bustling malls, Northwin Global City offers a diverse array of amenities catering to every aspect of modern living.

At Northwin Global City, sustainability is at the forefront of our vision. We are committed to implementing eco-conscious infrastructure and practices that prioritize the well-being of our community and the environment. From solar-powered streetlights to underground cabling systems and dedicated bike lanes, our development embraces innovative solutions to enhance connectivity while minimizing our ecological footprint. With a stormwater detention facility in place for flood prevention and an intermodal transport terminal facilitating seamless mobility, Northwin Global City is designed for the needs of today and tomorrow.

Conveniently spanning the municipalities of Marilao and Bocaue, Northwin Global City is strategically positioned for accessibility. Just minutes away from the Marilao Exit of NLEX, our location offers ease of travel for residents, commuters, and visitors alike. Additionally, our proximity to the proposed New Manila International Airport in Bulacan and the iconic Philippine Arena further enhances our connectivity and underscores our status as a burgeoning metropolitan center. Join us at Northwin Global City and experience the epitome of modern living in the heart of Bulacan.
