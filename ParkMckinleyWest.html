Park Mckinley West

Park Mckinley West is Megaworld's latest preselling condominium in its 34.5-hectare township of McKinley West. This township is located strategically in one of the 4 corners of Fort Bonifacio which makes it accessible to major central business districts such as Pasay City and Makati City. This condo is also just few meters away from the proposed Metro Manila Subway Station in Lawton Avenue which connects this condo to 4-5 different cities in Metro Manila including Ninoy Aquino International Airport Terminal 3.
