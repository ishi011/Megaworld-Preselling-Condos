About Uptown Modern

In a world that is continually evolving, the ability to adapt to the new normal is paramount, necessitating environments that not only anticipate but also embrace change. Uptown Modern stands as the latest addition to the high-rise residential landscape of Uptown Bonifacio, ushering in a new standard of contemporary living.

As the largest township developed by Megaworld in Fort Bonifacio, it encompasses the renowned Venice Grand Canal Mall, a hallmark of Metro Manila's urban landscape. Situated adjacent to exclusive enclaves such as Forbes Park and Dasmarinas Village, Uptown Modern pioneers the township concept in Fort Bonifacio. Inspired by the philosophies of influential global thinkers, Uptown Modern is crafted to epitomize future residential developments, prioritizing residents' comfort, convenience, and security.
